About me

Hi I'm Dorsa Zare. Art has always been a part of my life since I was a kid. I studied Performing Arts in high school and self learned digital arts and illustration. Further, I have a technical background in IT and programming. I obtained my Diploma of Vacational Studies (DVS) in Computer Support from Teccart Institute in Montreal and worked in the field for over two years. I am always passionate to learn and experience diffrent forms of art and design.

3D Modelling






Title of the work: Donut
Date of completion: 2022
Medium: Digital Art - 3Ds Max

Description: I used 3Ds max for creating this 3D model. The modeling process is briefly explained as follows. First, I sketched the model on paper. Second, I added the overall geometry and customized the shape and colors. Third, I changed the texture of the model. Last, I fixed the light and exposure before rendering.


Maquette






Title of the work: Wit Theatre Set Design Model
Date of completion: 2018
Size of work: (Width) 50 cm, (Length) 70 cm, (Hight) 20 cm
Medium: The main materials used are foam board, paper and wire.

Inspiration: This Maquette was a theater set design model for the play "Wit". Wit is a one-act play written by American playwright Margaret Edson, which illustrates the internal life of an English professor suffering a terminal illness in a hospital. As the professor nears the end of her life, she starts to ask hard questions about what she did wrong and also what comes next.
